Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about COR

When will COR be available?

COR is scheduled to launch on 1st July, 2025. Sign up for early access to be notified immediately when we go live and receive exclusive perks.

Will COR work offline?

Yes! We understand riders often travel through areas with limited connectivity. COR offers offline map access and essential features that work without an internet connection.

Is COR free to use?

COR will offer a free version with all essential features. Premium features will be available through a subscription model. Early access members will receive 6 months of premium features for free.

How many people can be in a riding group?

The free version will support groups of up to 5 riders. Premium users will be able to create and manage larger groups with enhanced tracking and communication features.
