What Makes COR Special

Discover the powerful features that will elevate your motorcycle touring experience.

Route Planning & Discovery

Discover scenic routes customised to your preferences or create custom routes with interactive checkpoints. Access popular destinations recommended by fellow riders.

Group Ride Management

Create and join group rides, assign roles, and stay connected with in-app messaging. Track real-time locations of group members during rides.

Expense Tracking

Track shared and personal expenses, categorize them by type, and split costs for group rides. Generate detailed expense reports after your journey.

Emergency Assistance

One-tap SOS feature, roadside assistance contacts, and automatic accident detection to keep you safe during your adventures.

Ride Analytics

Track routes, speed, distance, and riding duration. View detailed post-ride statistics and track your cumulative achievements.

Rider Community

Connect with like-minded riders, share routes and reviews, join challenges, and participate in local events and meetups.
